ğŸŒ¾ Wheat

Winter crop

  • Sowing: Oct - Dec
  • Harvest: Mar - Apr
  • Soil: Medium to loamy, good drainage
  • Water: 4 - 6 irrigations (approx.)
  • Seed rate: 100 - 120 kg/ha
  • Fertilizer: Basal DAP ~100 kg/ha; top-dress urea split doses
  • Pests/Diseases: Rust, leaf spots, aphids
  • Yield (est): 40 - 55 q/ha

ğŸŒ¾ Barley

Suitable in winter

  • Sowing: Oct - Dec
  • Harvest: Feb - Apr
  • Soil: Medium to light loamy
  • Water: Low to medium, 3 - 4 irrigations
  • Seed rate: 80 - 100 kg/ha
  • Fertilizer: Light NPK, emphasis on phosphorus
  • Problems: Fungal diseases, stem rot
  • Yield: 25 - 40 q/ha

ğŸŒ± Chickpea

Winter crop

  • Sowing: Oct - Nov
  • Harvest: Feb - Mar
  • Soil: Black soils preferred
  • Water: 1 - 2 irrigations usually after rain
  • Seed rate: 60 - 80 kg/ha
  • Fertilizer: Emphasize phosphorus
  • Pests/Diseases: Wilt, pod borer
  • Yield: 12 - 20 q/ha

ğŸŒ¿ Pea

Winter green peas

  • Sowing: Oct - Nov
  • Harvest: Jan - Feb
  • Soil: Light to medium, low alkalinity
  • Water: 3 - 4 irrigations
  • Seed rate: 80 - 120 kg/ha
  • Fertilizer: Low N, phosphorus required
  • Pests/Diseases: Powdery mildew, aphids
  • Yield: 30 - 40 q/ha (green pods)

ğŸŒ± Fenugreek

Winter seed crop

  • Sowing: Oct - Dec
  • Harvest: Jan - Feb
  • Soil: Medium to light, good organic matter
  • Water: 2 - 3 irrigations
  • Seed rate: 20 - 25 kg/ha
  • Fertilizer: Low N, moderate P
  • Problems: Fungal leaf spots
  • Yield: 6 - 12 q/ha

ğŸ§… Onion

Kharif & Winter varieties

  • Sowing: (Kharif) Jul - Aug, (Winter) Oct - Dec
  • Harvest: (Kharif) Oct - Nov, (Winter) Mar - Apr
  • Soil: Well-drained loamy soils
  • Water: 10 - 12 irrigations (regular)
  • Transplant: 45 - 50 day old nursery seedlings
  • Fertilizer: Balanced NPK + micro nutrients
  • Pests/Diseases: Thrips, purple blotch
  • Yield: 150 - 250 q/ha

ğŸ§„ Garlic

Clove-based planting

  • Sowing: Oct - Dec
  • Harvest: Apr
  • Soil: Light, rich in organic matter
  • Water: 8 - 10 irrigations
  • Planting material: 500 - 600 kg/ha (cloves)
  • Fertilizer: Balanced NPK + organics
  • Pests/Diseases: Purple blotch, thrips
  • Yield: 60 - 90 q/ha

ğŸ¥œ Groundnut (Peanut)

Summer crop

  • Sowing: Feb - Mar (region dependent)
  • Harvest: 4 - 5 months after sowing
  • Soil: Light sandy to medium, good drainage
  • Water: Moderate; avoid waterlogging
  • Seed rate: 60 - 80 kg/ha
  • Fertilizer: Emphasize P & K
  • Pests/Diseases: Pod borer, foliar diseases
  • Yield: 12 - 20 q/ha

ğŸŒ± Moong (Green gram)

Summer / Kharif varieties

  • Sowing: May - Jun (summer) / Jun - Jul (kharif)
  • Harvest: 60 - 90 days
  • Soil: Light, well-drained
  • Water: Low to moderate
  • Seed rate: 10 - 15 kg/ha
  • Fertilizer: Light N & P
  • Problems: Pod shattering, fungal issues
  • Yield: 6 - 10 q/ha

ğŸŒ³ Pigeon pea (Tuvar)

Summer / Kharif depending on area

  • Sowing: Region-specific (often summer/kharif)
  • Harvest: 6 - 9 months (variety dependent)
  • Soil: Black to medium soils
  • Water: Moderate
  • Seed rate: 10 - 30 kg/ha
  • Fertilizer: P & K focus
  • Pests/Diseases: Wilt, borers
  • Yield: 8 - 18 q/ha

ğŸŒ½ Maize

Summer & Kharif planting

  • Sowing: Summer Apr - Jun; Kharif Jun - Jul
  • Harvest: 3 - 4 months after sowing
  • Soil: Fertile, well-drained
  • Water: Moderate to high
  • Seed rate: 18 - 25 kg/ha
  • Fertilizer: High N with P-K
  • Pests/Diseases: Stem borer, rot
  • Yield: 30 - 80 q/ha

ğŸŒ¾ Sesame

Summer crop, drought tolerant

  • Sowing: Apr - May
  • Harvest: 3 - 4 months
  • Soil: Light sandy
  • Water: Low
  • Seed rate: 4 - 6 kg/ha
  • Fertilizer: P & K as required
  • Problems: Bacterial/fungal issues
  • Yield: 4 - 8 q/ha

ğŸŒ» Sunflower

Feb - Mar or Jun - Jul planting

  • Sowing: Feb - Mar or Jun - Jul
  • Harvest: 3 - 4 months
  • Soil: Medium to heavy, good drainage
  • Water: Moderate
  • Seed rate: 6 - 8 kg/ha
  • Fertilizer: NPK + micro elements
  • Problems: Downy mildew, heliothis
  • Yield: 8 - 18 q/ha

ğŸŒ± Sugarcane

Plantation crop (long duration)

  • Sowing: Feb - Jun or Oct - Dec depending on method
  • Harvest: 10 - 18 months (variety)
  • Soil: Loamy, deep soils
  • Water: High irrigation requirement
  • Seed rate: Setts/seed cane determined by system
  • Fertilizer: NPK + irrigation management
  • Problems: Ratoon issues, borers
  • Yield: 600 - 1000 t/ha (irrigated)

ğŸ§µ Cotton

Kharif main crop

  • Sowing: Jun - Jul (monsoon)
  • Harvest: Nov - Dec
  • Soil: Heavy with good organic matter
  • Water: 1 - 2 irrigations after rains typically
  • Seed rate: 12 - 20 kg/ha
  • Fertilizer: Balanced NPK + micro elements
  • Problems: Bollworm, fusarium
  • Yield: 8 - 20 q/ha fiber (variety dependent)

ğŸŒ¾ Urad (Black gram)

Kharif pulse

  • Sowing: Jun - Jul
  • Harvest: 70 - 100 days
  • Soil: Medium to light
  • Water: Moderate
  • Seed rate: 20 - 30 kg/ha
  • Problems: Anthracnose, root rot
  • Yield: 6 - 12 q/ha

ğŸŒ¾ Pearl Millet (Bajra)

Kharif drought-tolerant

  • Sowing: Jun - Jul
  • Harvest: 2.5 - 3.5 months
  • Soil: Light, arid soils okay
  • Water: Low
  • Seed rate: 6 - 8 kg/ha
  • Problems: Downy mildew, smut
  • Yield: 10 - 25 q/ha

ğŸŒ¾ Sorghum (Jowar)

Kharif / Summer

  • Sowing: Jun - Jul
  • Harvest: 3 - 5 months
  • Soil: Various
  • Water: Low to moderate
  • Seed rate: 8 - 12 kg/ha
  • Problems: Leaf blight, stem borer
  • Yield: 15 - 40 q/ha

ğŸŒ± Soybean

Kharif pulse / oilseed

  • Sowing: Jun - Jul
  • Harvest: 3 - 4 months
  • Soil: Medium to heavy with organic matter
  • Water: Moderate
  • Seed rate: 80 - 100 kg/ha
  • Fertilizer: Use bio-inoculants for N-fixation
  • Problems: Stem rot, pod borer
  • Yield: 10 - 25 q/ha

ğŸŒ¿ Coriander

Kharif / Winter options

  • Sowing: Jun - Jul (kharif) / Oct - Nov (winter)
  • Harvest: 30 - 75 days depending on variety
  • Soil: Medium, light loamy
  • Water: Moderate
  • Seed rate: 2 - 5 kg/ha
  • Problems: Damping off, leaf spot
  • Yield: 4 - 10 q/ha

ğŸŒ¿ Mustard / Rapeseed

Winter or some areas kharif

  • Sowing: Oct - Dec or Jun - Jul (in some areas)
  • Harvest: 3 - 4 months
  • Soil: Medium to light
  • Water: Low to moderate
  • Seed rate: 4 - 6 kg/ha
  • Problems: White rust, aphids
  • Yield: 6 - 12 q/ha
